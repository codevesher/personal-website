Research
Mini-Abstract

Then, both Floyd-Warshall Algorithm and Nearest Neighbor Algorithm were used to construct the shortest Hamiltonian circuit. However, assumptions regarding Hamiltonian optimality, scalability and generalisability issues reduced the method's reliability.

Enhancing Enzymatic Hydrolysis of Cotton-Based Textile-Waste
Research
Mini-Abstract
This study assessed Triton X-100 (surfactant) concentration effects on cellulase hydrolysis of pretreated cotton cellulose. DNSA spectrophotometry and Gaussian modelling revealed peak glucose yield at 0.0549% v/v. Thus, moderate non-ionic surfactant concentrations improve substrate accessibility and enzymatic efficiency, enhancing chemical textile recycling despite procedural limitations and pH conditions.
